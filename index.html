TutorSpace

Helping to bridge the gap in distance learning

Our Services are meant to help anyone who is a:


  • University and College students who are required to study remotely
  • Tutor offering tutoring services
  • Student who lacks the means or resources to self-study or study remotely efficiently

This platform aims to assist students and tutors by:


  • Providing Students with a platform to supplement their classtime learning
  • Giving tutors a medium to advertise and market their services during lockdown
  • Provide students struggling to study remotely with an opportunity to get assistance in a more personal and attentive setting than an online classroom

Tutor Services:


  • Reviewing curiccula topics and assignments
  • Note taking strategies
  • Grasp new concepts
  • Exam preparations
